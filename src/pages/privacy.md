Blurb is a minimalist digital business card application designed to help users share their website or social links quickly through QR codes during in-person interactions. This Privacy Policy explains what information Blurb collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

## 1. Information Collected

Blurb is designed to minimize data collection. The app only collects or stores information necessary for its core functionality.

### Information You Provide

When using Blurb, you may create cards containing:

* Website URLs
* Social media links
* Optional titles or labels for your cards

This information is stored solely for the purpose of generating and displaying QR codes for sharing.

### Automatically Collected Information

Blurb does not collect personal information such as:

* Phone numbers
* Email addresses
* Contact lists
* Device contacts

The app does not track or log who scans your QR codes.

---

## 2. How Information Is Used

Information stored in Blurb is used only to:

* Save links you add as cards
* Generate QR codes corresponding to those links
* Display your cards within the app

Blurb does not analyze, sell, or use this information for advertising or marketing.

---

## 3. Data Storage

Depending on the implementation of the app:

* Card data may be stored locally on your device.
* If cloud storage or account features are added in the future, users will be notified and this policy will be updated.

---

## 4. Third-Party Links

Cards created in Blurb may contain links to external websites or social media platforms. When someone scans your QR code and opens a link, they are directed to that external service.

Blurb is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites or platforms.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

Blurb does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

The only information shared is the link encoded in the QR code when a user chooses to display it for others to scan.

---

## 6. Data Security

Blurb takes reasonable steps to protect stored data. However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

Users should ensure that any links they add to cards are safe and appropriate to share publicly.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.
