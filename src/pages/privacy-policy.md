Blurb is privacy-first by default. This is a placeholder privacy policy page.

## What we collect

- Basic account details you choose to provide.
- Links you save as cards.
- Usage analytics to improve the app experience.

## What we do not collect

- Your personal phone number unless you explicitly add it.
- Contact lists from your device.
- Any data unrelated to your cards or profile.

## How we use data

We use the information above to operate the app, improve reliability, and help you manage your cards. We do not sell personal data.
