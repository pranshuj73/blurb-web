# Iteration 2 — Flow Section

## Observations
- The flow panels read well but feel like “cards on a rail” instead of a narrative strip.
- Visuals are functional but not as direct as the copy; they should mirror the actual app steps more explicitly.
- The step numbers can do more hierarchy work.

## Updates
- Tightened panel typography and spacing.
- Made step numbers more prominent and aligned to a consistent baseline.
- Increased visual contrast between steps to create a clearer scanning rhythm.
