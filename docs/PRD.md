# Product Requirements Document — Blurb Landing Website

**Project:** Blurb Landing Website  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-03-31

## Objective
Create a landing page that clearly communicates the purpose of Blurb in under five seconds, demonstrates the product interaction visually, and converts visitors into interested users or contacts. The page must emphasize clarity, minimal friction, and real-world utility.

## Target Audience
**Primary:**
- Developers
- Designers
- Freelancers
- Students
- Startup founders
- People attending meetups or conferences

**Secondary:**
- Recruiters
- Creators
- Network-driven professionals

## Core Message
Blurb allows users to share their website or social profile instantly using a QR code.

**Key benefit:** Remove friction from in-person introductions.

## Success Metrics
- Landing comprehension time < 5 seconds
- Bounce rate < 35%
- Time on page > 40 seconds
- Scroll completion > 60%
- Conversion: contact clicks

## Information Hierarchy
1. What Blurb is
2. How it works
3. Why it matters
4. Visual proof
5. Contact / follow-up
